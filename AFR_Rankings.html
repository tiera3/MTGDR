Click on a color symbol above to centre on that color, and to choose to order by allied or enemy pairs. The right-hand columns is for colorless, and multi-colored cards.
The search box in the top right is used to locate a specific card. The sort box below it allows you to choose which critera to sort by (Premier Draft info from 17lands).
